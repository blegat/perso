High level toolbox

  • EntropicCone.jl: Approximation of the Entropic Cone and Optimization over it.
  • SwitchedSystems.jl: Stability analysis and control synthesis for Switched Systems.

Optimization algorithms

  • SumOfSquares.jl: Sum of Squares restriction and Moment relaxation for PolyJuMP.jl: Modelling with polynomial and measure variables in JuMP.
  • StochasticDualDynamicProgramming.jl: Stochastic Dual Dynamic Programming algorithm for StructJuMP.jl: Block-structured optimization framework for JuMP.

Utility

  • Polyhedra.jl: Polyhedral computation framework with support for CDD, LRS, QHull and ConvexHull.jl.
  • MultivariatePolynomials.jl: Manipulating sparse polynomials, the moments of measures and (semi)algebraic sets.
